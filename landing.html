About CTU E-Clinic

CTU E-Clinic is a modern web-based platform designed specifically for Cebu Technological University students to manage their healthcare appointments and medical queries efficiently. Our system aims to reduce wait times, improve accessibility, and ensure quality healthcare services for all students.
